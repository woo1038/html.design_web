Shareware (trial version, normal trial version, shareware, culture language: shareware, postpay software, payment program) is one of the marketing methods of computer software. Also called demoware and evaluation software. Shareware software is usually available free of charge from the Internet or through a disc included in the magazine. Users can try out the program. Shareware implies "Try it before you buy it." The shareware program displays a purchase requisition message, and software distribution licenses require such a payment method. Usually, such software is limited in terms of use and function.
